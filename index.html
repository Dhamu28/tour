South Zone

Location: Below the Vindhya Range
Major Cities: Chennai, Hyderabad, Bangalore, Cochin, Pondicherry
Holy Destinations: Rameshwaram, Sabarimala, Thiruvananthapuram
Holiday Destinations: Alappuzha Backwaters, Southern Beaches, Lakshadweep

East Zone

Location: To the east of Son and Kosi rivers
Major Cities: Patna, Kolkata, Aizwal, Shillong

West Zone

Major Cities: Mumbai, Panjim, Pune, Ahmedabad, Nashik, Khajuraho
Heritage Destinations: Daman & Diu, Patan, Mahabaleshwar
Holiday Destinations: Goa Beaches, Ajanta & Ellora Caves, Khajuraho Temples
